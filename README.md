7. Key Findings

•	Research & Development has the highest number of employee exits. 

•	Sales has the highest attrition rate. 

•	Employees aged 26–35 years are more likely to leave. 

•	Overtime significantly increases attrition. 

•	Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have higher turnover. 

•	Lower income is associated with higher attrition. 

•	Lower job satisfaction contributes to resignations. 

•	Single employees have higher attrition. 

•	Frequent business travel increases employee turnover. 

•	Employees with fewer years of experience are more likely to resign.
